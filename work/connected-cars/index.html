Connected Cars

In January 2017, I joined a Danish startup called Connected Cars, who connect cars to the cloud and offer a product where car owners can stay on top of their car's health and give them a direct channel to workshops. Car owners should have peace of mind and get technical assistance without having to understand the technical intricacies of a modern vehicle. Read more about it at connectedcars.io

The possibility to shape and design an ambitious system from the ground was an opportunity I couldn't turn down. I joined as the fifth employee - as the first designer - when the company was four months old tasked with performing user research, usability tests and designing wireframes.

Since then I've drawn at least a thousand wireframes on whiteboards, designed UI for both web and mobile, learned some React and React-Native and committed production code. If you want to try it all I'd recommend a startup.

I have defined user journeys for both of workshop and car owner products during the initial work on our MVP. Check out this small case study about design documentation and facilitating discussions within the team.

We launched our first MVP product, MinVolkswagen, in June 2017 and in our first year we've got three more brands and more than 60000 cars connected to our platform.
