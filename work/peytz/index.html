Peytz & Co

I joined Peytz & Co after finishing my master's degree in the summer of 2016 as a UX consultant. I got to work closely with multiple clients doing design, research and a bit of strategy.

Peytz & Co are a full-service digital agency specializing in Drupal and Wordpress sites with most of their clients are in media or the public sector. I worked in the creative department where I worked tightly with graphic designers and front-end developers.

Due to my background focusing primarily on research I was brought in to do usability tests, user interviews, and analysis. However, in the first two months, there weren't many research tasks, so this is where I started to do more and more design work creating wireframes and prototypes.
